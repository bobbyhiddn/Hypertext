## Word
**FAITH** — Complete trust, confidence, or reliance; the assurance of things hoped for.

## Old Testament
> Habakkuk 2:4 — “But the just shall live by his faith.”

## New Testament
> Hebrews 11:1 — “Now faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen.”

## Trivia
- The Hebrew root 'aman' implies firmness or support and also gives us the word 'Amen'.
- Pistis appears 243 times in the New Testament, appearing most frequently in Romans.
- Hebrews 11 is famously known as the 'Hall of Faith', listing historical figures who trusted God.
- In the Old Testament, the concept often emphasizes faithfulness, steadfastness, and reliability rather than abstract belief.
