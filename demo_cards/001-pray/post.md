## Word
**PRAY** — To address God with adoration, confession, supplication, or thanksgiving.

## Old Testament
> 2 Chronicles 7:14 — “If my people... shall humble themselves, and pray, and seek my face...”

## New Testament
> 1 Thessalonians 5:17 — “Pray without ceasing.”

## Trivia
- The Hebrew verb *palal* implies 'intervening' or 'judging', suggesting prayer bridges a gap.
- The Greek *proseuchomai* combines *pros* (towards) and *euchomai* (to wish or vow).
- Forms of the word 'pray' appear over 300 times in most English translations.
- The first use of *palal* is Abraham praying for Abimelech in Genesis 20:7.
- Jesus' longest recorded prayer is found in John 17.
