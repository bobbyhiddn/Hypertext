## Word
**PRAY** — To address God with adoration, confession, supplication, or thanksgiving.

## Old Testament
> 2 Chronicles 7:14 — “if my people... will humble themselves and pray and seek my face...”

## New Testament
> 1 Thessalonians 5:17 — “pray without ceasing”

## Trivia
- The Hebrew verb 'palal' implies intervention, judgment, or arbitration.
- The Greek 'proseuchomai' combines 'pros' (towards) and 'euchomai' (to wish or vow).
- Incense is frequently used in Scripture as a metaphor for the prayers of the saints.
- Depending on the translation, forms of this word appear over 600 times in the Bible.
