## Word
**MOSES** — The great prophet, lawgiver, and deliverer of Israel who led the Exodus and received the Torah.

## Old Testament
> Deuteronomy 34:10 — "Since then, no prophet has risen in Israel like Moses, whom the Lord knew face to face."

## New Testament
> John 1:17 — "For the law was given through Moses; grace and truth came through Jesus Christ."

## Trivia
- His name means 'drawn out' in Hebrew, as he was pulled from the Nile by Pharaoh's daughter.
- Traditionally credited with writing the first five books of the Bible, known as the Pentateuch.
- Lived exactly 120 years: 40 in Egypt, 40 in Midian, and 40 leading Israel in the wilderness.
- The only person in the Old Testament said to speak with God 'face to face' as a friend.
