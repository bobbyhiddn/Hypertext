## Word
**MOSES** — Drawn out; to pull out or rescue

## Old Testament
> Exodus 2:10 — "She called his name Moses: and she said, Because I drew him out of the water."

## New Testament
> John 1:17 — "For the law was given by Moses, but grace and truth came by Jesus Christ."

## Trivia
- His name means 'drawn out,' referencing his rescue from the Nile.
- Traditionally credited with writing the Torah (the first five books of the Bible).
- Lived 120 years: 40 as a prince, 40 as a shepherd, and 40 as a leader.
- Spoke with God 'face to face' as a man speaks to his friend.
