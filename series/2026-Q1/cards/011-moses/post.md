## Word
**MOSES** — To draw out; the deliverer, lawgiver, and prophet of Israel who led the Exodus.

## Old Testament
> Exodus 2:10 — "She named him Moses, saying, 'I drew him out of the water.'"

## New Testament
> John 1:17 — "For the law was given through Moses; grace and truth came through Jesus Christ."

## Trivia
- Born to Amram and Jochebed of the tribe of Levi.
- His name sounds like the Hebrew verb 'mashah', meaning 'to draw out'.
- Lived 120 years: 40 in Egypt, 40 in Midian, 40 in the wilderness.
- Spoke with God 'face to face' as a man speaks with his friend.
- Traditionally attributed as the author of the Pentateuch (Torah).
