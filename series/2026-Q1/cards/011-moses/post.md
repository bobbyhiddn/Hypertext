## Word
**MOSES** — The great prophet, lawgiver, and deliverer who led Israel out of Egypt.

## Old Testament
> Exodus 33:11 — "The Lord would speak to Moses face to face, as one speaks to a friend."

## New Testament
> John 1:17 — "For the law was given through Moses; grace and truth came through Jesus Christ."

## Trivia
- His name sounds like the Hebrew verb 'mashah', meaning 'to draw out'.
- Lived 120 years: 40 in Egypt, 40 in Midian, and 40 in the wilderness.
- The only person in the Bible described as speaking with God 'face to face'.
- Traditionally credited with writing the Pentateuch (the first five books of the Bible).
- Appeared alongside Elijah during the Transfiguration of Jesus.
