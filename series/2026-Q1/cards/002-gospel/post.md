## Word
**GOSPEL** — The proclamation of good news; specifically the message of salvation through Jesus Christ.

## Old Testament
> Isaiah 52:7 — “How beautiful upon the mountains are the feet of him who brings good news.”

## New Testament
> Romans 1:16 — “For I am not ashamed of the gospel, for it is the power of God for salvation.”

## Trivia
- Derived from the Old English 'godspell', literally meaning 'good story' or 'good news'.
- In secular ancient Greek, the word was used for announcements of military victory or an emperor's birth.
- Paul defines the core content of the gospel in 1 Corinthians 15 as Christ's death, burial, and resurrection.
- The Hebrew equivalent is rooted in the idea of a messenger bringing fresh tidings from the battlefront.
