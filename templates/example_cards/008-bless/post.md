## Word
**BLESS** — To confer prosperity, happiness, or well-being; to consecrate; to praise.

## Old Testament
> Numbers 6:24 — "The LORD bless you and keep you..."

## New Testament
> Matthew 5:9 — "Blessed are the peacemakers, for they will be called children of God."

## Trivia
- The Hebrew 'barak' literally means 'to kneel,' implying a gift given or received on bended knee.
- The Greek 'eulogeo' means 'to speak well of,' which gives us the English word 'eulogy.'
- The Priestly Blessing (Num 6:24) was found on silver scrolls dating to 600 BC, the oldest biblical text ever discovered.
- In Scripture, a blessing is an irrevocable endowment of vitality and power, not merely a wish for good luck.
