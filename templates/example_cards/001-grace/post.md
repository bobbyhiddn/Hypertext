## Word
**GRACE** — Unmerited favor; a free gift given to the undeserving

## Old Testament
> Genesis 6:8 — "But Noah found grace in the eyes of the LORD."

## New Testament
> Ephesians 2:8 — "For by grace you have been saved through faith... it is the gift of God."

## Trivia
- The Greek word 'charis' is the root of the English word 'charisma,' meaning 'gift.'
- Hebrew 'chen' implies a superior stooping down in kindness to show favor to an inferior.
- Often remembered by the acronym: God's Riches At Christ's Expense.
- The name John (Yochanan) means 'Yahweh is gracious.'
